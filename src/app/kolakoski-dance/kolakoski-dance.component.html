Rules
•If the sequence passes from 1 to 1 or from 2 to 2 (i.e. it stays in the same box): rotate of 30°:
M =

cos 30°

-sin 30°

sin 30°

cos 30°
•If the sequence passes from 1 to 2 or from 2 to 1 (i.e. it changes box): reflect over the y-axis of the triangle, interchanging the colors.
M =

cos θ

sin θ

sin θ

-cos θ
Where θ=n*30 and n is the number of times the rotation was applied.
